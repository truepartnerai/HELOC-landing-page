Common Questions

Straight answers.

What exactly are you offering contractors?
I'm a licensed mortgage broker. When you have a customer who needs financing for their project, refer them to me. I'll work with them directly to get a fast home equity line of credit — no appraisal needed, often funded in 3 days. You don't have to wait for them to figure out their own bank financing.
Do I get paid for referrals?
No. This isn't a paid referral program — federal lending regulations don't allow it. The value to you is simple: more jobs close when your customers can access funding fast, instead of stalling out trying to navigate traditional bank financing.
What's my role after I refer someone?
You're hands-off. Once you connect us, I work directly with your customer. They make all the lending decisions on their own. You don't sell the loan, quote rates, or advise on financing — just point them my way and stay focused on your project.
How fast can my customer actually get funded?
In many cases, funding can happen in as little as 3 business days from application. No appraisal required, automated underwriting, and the customer chooses their own rate/cost combination. Compare that to traditional bank HELOCs, which typically take 45 to 60 days.
Does my customer have to give up their low first mortgage rate?
No. This is a second-lien home equity product. Their existing first mortgage stays untouched — including any low rate they locked in over the past few years. That's a big part of why customers say yes to this where they wouldn't say yes to a refinance.
What if my customer doesn't qualify?
Not every customer will qualify — minimum 620 FICO, up to 89% combined loan-to-value, and a few other underwriting requirements. If this specific product doesn't fit, I have access to plenty of other mortgage and home equity options through my wholesale network, and I'll work with them to find something that does.
Is this available outside Utah?
TruePartner Home Loans is currently licensed in Utah, Texas, Florida, and Idaho. If your customer is in another state, reach out — additional states are being added regularly.
